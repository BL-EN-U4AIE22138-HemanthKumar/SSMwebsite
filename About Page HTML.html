Introducing Shri S.S. Mallikarjun

Minister of Horticulture, Mines and Geology

Shri S.S. Mallikarjun is an esteemed leader celebrated for his unwavering dedication to sustainable development, grassroots empowerment, and visionary governance. Presently serving as Karnataka’s Minister for Mines & Geology and Horticulture, he has consistently championed the harmonious integration of economic advancement with environmental conservation.

Shri S.S. Mallikarjun is an esteemed leader celebrated for his unwavering dedication to sustainable development, grassroots empowerment, and visionary governance. Presently serving as Karnataka’s Minister for Mines & Geology and Horticulture, he has consistently championed the harmonious integration of economic advancement with environmental conservation.

Born on September 22, 1967, in Davanagere, Karnataka, Shri Mallikarjun's entry into public service was deeply motivated by his aspiration to address the pressing challenges confronting rural communities. With a distinguished political career spanning over two decades, he has emerged as a beacon of progressive leadership, spearheading Karnataka’s transformation into a center of eco-conscious growth and agricultural excellence.

A staunch advocate for sustainable mining, Shri Mallikarjun has pioneered innovative policies aimed at minimizing environmental degradation while fostering economic prosperity. Concurrently, his dynamic contributions to horticulture have empowered countless communities, driving agricultural innovation and ensuring livelihoods are enriched through sustainable practices.
