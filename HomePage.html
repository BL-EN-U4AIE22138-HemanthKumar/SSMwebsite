    Meet Shri S.S. Mallikarjun

    Shri S.S. Mallikarjun is a seasoned leader, renowned for his unwavering commitment to sustainable development, grassroots empowerment, and innovative governance.     Born on September 22, 1967, in Davanagere, Karnataka, Shri Mallikarjun's journey into public service was inspired by his desire to address the challenges faced by rural communities. With over two decades of political experience, he has become a symbol of progressive leadership, transforming Karnataka into a hub for eco-friendly development and agricultural innovation. As a passionate advocate for sustainable mining, Shri Mallikarjun has introduced groundbreaking policies to mitigate environmental impacts while ensuring economic growth. Simultaneously, his efforts in horticulture have empowered thousands of farmers, enabling them to adopt modern agricultural practices and secure better livelihoods.
