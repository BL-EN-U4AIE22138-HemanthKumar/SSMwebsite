    Meet Shri S.S. Mallikarjun

    Shri S.S. Mallikarjun, a dynamic leader and dedicated public servant, currently serves as the Minister for Mines & Geology and Horticulture in Karnataka. Committed to sustainable development, he champions eco-friendly practices, agricultural growth, and rural upliftment across the state.
