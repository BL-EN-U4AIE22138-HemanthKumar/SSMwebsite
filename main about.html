About Me

Meet Shri S.S. Mallikarjun

Shri S.S. Mallikarjun, a visionary leader and advocate for sustainable progress, embodies transformative leadership in Karnataka. As the Minister for Mines & Geology and Horticulture, he skillfully balances economic growth with environmental stewardship. Born on September 22, 1967, in Davanagere, Karnataka, his dedication to rural empowerment and innovation defines his over 20-year political career.

A pioneer of sustainable mining, he has implemented policies that merge economic development with ecological preservation. In horticulture, his initiatives have revolutionized farming, uplifting thousands of farmers through modern practices and improved livelihoods.

Key Contributions

Empowering Farmers and Boosting Productivity

Shri Mallikarjun has significantly increased the production of fruits, vegetables, flowers, and spices, making Karnataka a horticultural leader. His support for Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) has enabled farmers to access quality resources, technology, and fair market linkages, directly improving their incomes and livelihoods.

Infrastructure and Market Development

Focused on sustainability, he has prioritized irrigation in drought-prone areas, built cold storage facilities, and developed post-harvest infrastructure to minimize losses. His efforts to promote high-value crops and enhance export readiness have strengthened Karnataka’s position in global horticulture markets.

Training and Modern Techniques

Through training programs, Shri Mallikarjun has empowered farmers with advanced techniques like organic farming and drip irrigation, fostering sustainable practices and increasing agricultural efficiency.
